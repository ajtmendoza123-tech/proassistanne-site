My Story

How I Got Here

My career journey has been anything but ordinary. I began as a medical technologist, a foundation that gave me precision, attention to detail, and a deep respect for accuracy. That discipline never left me.

In 2017, I made a bold move to the UAE, working as a Document Controller for engineering firms. Living abroad sharpened my professionalism and gave me the confidence to work with international clients at the highest level.

Returning to the Philippines in 2019, I transitioned into Virtual Assistance, and I have never looked back. By 2022, I had earned a promotion to Operational Excellence Manager, a title that reflects the trust my clients place in me every day.

Always Growing

Life Beyond the Desk

By night, I am a passionate gamer and live streamer on Facebook, TikTok, Twitch, and YouTube. Gaming taught me strategy, patience, and thinking three steps ahead, skills I bring to every project.

I have a deep love for photography, capturing quiet moments, golden light, and places rarely noticed. I share this journey on Instagram @kawaiianne.journal and Pinterest FramedByKawaiianne.

I also love cooking and baking. I once ran my own food business called Yummy Box, selling homemade fried rice that my family and customers absolutely loved, an experience that shaped how I think about care, consistency, and delivery.

My motto: Stay curious, keep growing, and enjoy the ride!

Quick Q&A

Get to Know Me Better

The kind of things you'd ask over coffee. ☕

Why did you become a Virtual Assistant?
I wanted flexibility, freedom, and the chance to work with amazing people from all over the world. It turned out I was really good at making other people's lives easier!
What's your superpower as a VA?
Anticipating what needs to be done before being asked. I don't just complete tasks. I think ahead, flag issues early, and bring structure to even the most chaotic workflows.
What's your work setup like?
I work from my home office in the Philippines with a reliable high-speed connection, dual monitors, and a quiet space fully dedicated to client work. I'm built for deep focus.
What kind of clients do you work best with?
Business owners and entrepreneurs who value quality over quantity, communicate clearly, and want a long-term partner, not just someone to tick boxes. Mutual respect is everything.
If you weren't a VA, what would you be?
Honestly? A professional photographer or a pastry chef. Creativity and precision run through everything I do. It just happens that I found the perfect outlet in virtual assistance.
What's one thing most people don't know about you?
I used to run a small food business called Yummy Box, selling homemade fried rice that people absolutely loved. That experience taught me as much about business as any VA course.
What do you do when you're not working?
I'm gaming, streaming, editing photos, trying new recipes, or planning my next trip. I genuinely believe a full life outside work makes you a better, more creative professional.
What's your favourite destination so far?
Taiwan, Thailand, and the UAE are all so special to me in different ways. Taiwan for the food and warmth, Thailand for the beauty, and the UAE for shaping who I am professionally. I truly cannot pick just one!
